Enrollment Is Currently Closed

During open enrollment, individuals and families may change health care or dental plans and add or drop coverage for family members. This is the only time individuals may enroll in or change a health plan.These changes are not possible at other times unless an individual experiences a qualifying event, such as marriage, divorce, birth, adoption, or loss of other health insurance coverage. Those that have a qualifying event may need to submit documentation regarding their event.

Have a Qualifying Event?

If you have a qualifying event, you are required to register with your insurer within 30 days and apply for coverage within 60 days of the qualifying event.
